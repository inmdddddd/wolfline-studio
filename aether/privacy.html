Privacy policy.

Last updated: 5 July 2026. What is collected, why, and the rights you hold over it.

1. The data controller

The company operating this site is the controller of the personal data collected through it. Its registered details are provided on the order confirmation and invoice. For anything concerning your data, write to contact@aether-studio.example.

2. What is collected

Account data (name, email, encrypted password), order data (name, email, telephone, delivery address) and technical traffic data (IP address, page requested, time of visit) — detailed in section 8. No card details are collected on this site.

Account data

Name, email, encrypted password — when you create an account.

Order data

Name, email, telephone, delivery address — when you place an order.

Payment

No card details are collected or stored on this site. Settlement is arranged at delivery, where available.

Technical data

IP address, page and time of visit — on each request. See section 8.

3. Why it is collected

Your data is used solely to process orders, run your account, answer support requests and meet legal obligations (accounting, returns). It is not sold or rented to third parties for marketing.

4. Who receives it

Delivery data may be passed to the courier completing your delivery. Nothing is shared with other third parties beyond what is strictly necessary to deliver the order.

5. How long it is kept

Account data is kept while your account is active. Order data is kept as long as accounting law requires. You may request deletion of your account at any time, except for data we are legally bound to keep.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, rectification, deletion or restriction of the processing of your data, object to processing, or request portability. For any such request, write to contact@aether-studio.example.

7. Cookies

Full detail on the cookies used is in the Cookie policy.

8. Technical data and traffic measurement (IP address)

Beyond account and order data, a few technical details are recorded automatically on each visit: IP address, page requested, time of visit and referrer. No tracking cookies are used for this — the server records it directly. The purpose is strictly internal: the data is read in aggregate (for example, which regions most orders come from) for business decisions. It is not used for targeted advertising and is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing. The legal basis is our legitimate interest in understanding and improving the business. You may request information about this data, or its deletion, at contact@aether-studio.example.
