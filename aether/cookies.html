Cookie policy.

Last updated: 5 July 2026. This site uses the minimum: only cookies strictly required for it to function.

1. What a cookie is

A cookie is a small text file kept by your browser that lets the site remember things between visits — that you are signed in, or what you are holding in your cart.

2. The cookies used here

Only cookies strictly necessary for the site to function. No marketing cookies, no advertising cookies, no third-party analytics.

beca_session

Keeps you signed in to your account. Expires automatically after 7 days.

beca_cart

Keeps the pieces in your cart between visits. Expires automatically after 30 days.

3. Why there is no consent banner

The cookies above are strictly necessary for the account and the cart to work. Under the law applicable to technical cookies, they do not require a separate consent banner.

4. Controlling cookies

You can delete or block cookies from your browser settings at any time. Blocking the strictly necessary ones will affect how the account and the cart work.

5. Traffic measurement (no cookies)

Beyond the cookies above, the server records a few technical details on each visit — IP address, page requested, time of visit — without using any cookie. This is used internally for traffic and order statistics, never for advertising. Full detail in the Privacy policy, section 8.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The version in force is always the one published on this page.
