Returns

The right of return.

Last updated: 5 July 2026. If a piece is not right for you, this is how it comes back. The edition itself does not change — a returned piece keeps its number.

1. The right

You may return a piece within 14 calendar days of receiving your order, without giving a reason, as consumer protection law provides.

2. Condition

Pieces must be unworn, unwashed, with all original labels attached and, where possible, in their original packaging. These are fixed-edition garments — a damaged or worn piece cannot re-enter its edition and cannot be accepted.

3. How to begin

Write to contact@aether-studio.example with your order number (format BC-0000) and the piece you are returning. Instructions follow within 24 working hours.

4. Reimbursement

Once the returned piece is received and verified, reimbursement is made within 14 calendar days at most, through the same means used for the order.

5. Return carriage

Return carriage terms are confirmed with your return instructions.
