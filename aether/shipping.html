Shipping

How a piece reaches you.

Last updated: 5 July 2026. Every order is prepared by hand and handed to the courier — here is what happens after you place it.

Coverage

Delivery zones are confirmed with your order by email.

Time

2–5 working days from confirmation

Cost

Communicated with your order confirmation.

1. Preparation

Once your order is confirmed, it is prepared and handed to the courier within 1–2 working days. You receive an email with your order number immediately after checkout.

2. Carriage

Your tracking number is sent by email as soon as the order ships.

3. Delays

The times above are estimates. They can vary with the courier, the season and your location. We do not answer for delays caused by events outside our direct control.

4. If something goes wrong

If tracking shows delivered but nothing arrived, or the piece arrived damaged, write to contact@aether-studio.example with your order number as soon as possible.
